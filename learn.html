Value at risk analysis estimates the magnitude of large, frequently occurring losses which help describe a portfolio's risk.

Reliability

Varify generates several value at risk models with varying parameters; identifying the most accurate model.

Models which are successfully backtested ensure a high degree of reliability going forward, which is reflected by a low p-value result.

Benchmark

As a comparison, keep in mind, the Basel Accord allows up to 4 breaches over a sample size of 250 99th percentile VaRs which is the equivalent of a 38% p-value.

Requirements

The dropped file should contain

  • daily prices sorted from the most recent date down
  • a column named 'Adjusted Close'
  • a sufficient history to make statistical inferences (i.e. at the very least 1500 prices for 99th; 500 for the 95th percentile and so on)
